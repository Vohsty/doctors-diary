The Doctors

Primary care isn’t just about getting treatment when you’re sick; it’s about taking control of your overall health and wellness. Building a relationship with a primary care doctor keeps you proactive about your health. Primary care physicians can detect and treat a variety of conditions, from chronic or infectious diseases to mental health problems. Their medical expertise and devotion to good health and prevention provides a well-rounded approach to healthcare.

Our Services

Intensive Care Unit (ICU)

The Intensive Care Unit (ICU) is positioned in close proximity to operating theatres and catered for those who require intensive monitoring, nursing care and complex respiratory support.

Laboratory

We offer many radiology, diagnostic services and tests in one location, minimal waiting, highly trained staff and exceptional service for physicians and patients.

Ambulance Service

Here at Skyfall our Ambulance teams are trained by Health Care Professionals to ensure they provide the highest level of care. Our aim is to ensure your journey with us is as smooth as can be with minimal disruption to your normal daily life.

Radiology

Our radiology specialists provide accurate diagnostic imaging reports to assist your doctor in making the best decisions for your treatment.

Emergency Care

Walk-ins same day, urgent care for non-life threatening injuries and illnesses that need immediate attention.

Pharmacy

The pharmacy is committed to providing efficient services to both in-patients and out-patients, whilst ensuring that treatment is safe and appropriate. We dispense quality medicines at all our pharmacy outlets at the best price possible.
