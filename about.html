Our Hospital

Skyfall plaza is a private hospital which strives to promote the health of the population by providing accessible, affordable and appropriate quality clinical services to our patients.


We offer in and out-patient services to individuals and corporates. Insurance companies in our panel include JUBILEE, CIC, AON, APA, AAR, UAP, BRITAM, MADISON, RESOLUTION and REAL INSURANCE. We also offer services to walk-in cash paying clients.


We are also an accredited provider for the NHIF civil servants scheme.


The services offered in our hospital include:
  • 24 hour Outpatient Doctor’s consultation
  • Laboratory services
  • Operating Theatre
  • Specialist clinics
  • Medical examinations (for Insurance underwriting/pre-employment/general medical examinations).

We also provide value added services in a friendly and comfortable environment.
