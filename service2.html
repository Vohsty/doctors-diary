Radiotherapy

This treatment is delivered by a linear accelerator.It mainly controls or kills the cancer cells reducing the chances of cancer coming back to help reduce symptoms.

It uses high energy rays to destroy cancer cells.It is issued in two different ways.

  • External Radiotherapy

    • A machine directs beams of radiation at the cancer.

  • Internal Radiotherapy

    • A radioactive implant is placed inside your body near the cancer or it is swallowed or injected.

Chemotherapy

The type of treatment uses one or more anti-cancer drugs as part of standardized chemotherapy regimen.It can be given with a curative intentor aim to reduce sypmtoms.

Physiotherapy

It mainly focuses on the physical exercise of the muscles.This helps in strenghening the muscles and improving the function,preventing pain and injury.

Immunotherapy

The treatment boosts the body's natural defenses to fight cancer.It activates or suppresses the immune system.

Hormone based therapy

The treatment slows or stops the growth of cancer that uses hormones to grow mainly Prostate and breast cancer.
